Study Tips

schedule Time Management

  • Study in 25-minute focused sessions (Pomodoro Technique)
  • Take short 5-minute breaks between sessions
  • After 4 sessions, take a longer 15-30 minute break

psychology Effective Learning

  • Review material within 24 hours of learning it
  • Teach concepts to someone else to reinforce understanding
  • Connect new information to things you already know

self_improvement Wellness

  • Get 7-9 hours of sleep each night
  • Stay hydrated throughout your study sessions
  • Take regular physical activity breaks
