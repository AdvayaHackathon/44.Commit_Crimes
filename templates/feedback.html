Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password.

Can I download content for offline learning?

Yes, most content can be downloaded for offline use. Look for the download icon next to the content you want to save.

How do I track my progress?

Your progress is automatically tracked and displayed on your dashboard. You can also view detailed analytics in the Test & Analysis section.

Can I change my learning plan?

Yes, you can customize your learning plan in the Planner section. You can add, remove, or reschedule learning activities.

How do I get help with a specific subject?

You can ask your Personal AI Coach for help with any subject. Simply navigate to the Coach section and ask your question.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your personal information and learning data are encrypted and stored securely.
